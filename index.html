Information

We do one special flavour of cronuts per month. We begin offering freshly made Cronut® pastries every morning when the bakery opens at 8am (9am Sundays). We recommend arriving early!
As with most of our desserts, it is best to enjoy your Cronut® pastries as soon as possible so they are fresh, as they have a short shelf life of just 6-8 hours.
If you are bringing your Cronut® pastries home, please do not refrigerate! The humidity from the refrigerator will make them soggy.
Since Cronut® pastries are filled with cream, do not re-heat or serve them warm or hot, as the ganache filling will liquefy.
And if you do wish to cut your Cronut®, please use a serrated knife, so as not to crush the layers.
